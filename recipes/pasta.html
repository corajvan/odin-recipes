Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

This delightful Fettuccine Alfredo recipe is a great staple for those nights when you just don't want to cook.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Fettuccine pasta
  • 6 tablespoons butter
  • 1 garlic clove (minced)
  • 1 1/2 cups heavy cream
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/4 cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons parsley

Instructions

  1. In a large pot, heat water over high heat until boiling. Add salt to season the water once it comes to a boil. Once it is boiling, add fettuccine and cook according to package instructions. Save about 1/3 cup of the pasta water
  2. In a large skillet or pan, heat butter over medium heat. Add minced garlic and cook for 1 to 2 minutes. Stir in heavy cream.
  3. Let heavy cream reduce and cook for 5 to 8 minutes. Add half of the parmesan cheese to the mixture and whisk well until smooth. Keep over heat and whisk well until cheese is melted.
  4. Add some of the reserved pasta water to the sauce. Toss alfredo sauce with fettuccine pasta and add half of the parmesan cheese. Once it is tossed, garnish with the remaining parmesan cheese. Add a little more pasta water if it needs to be thinned out.
  5. Garnish with parsley, if desired.
