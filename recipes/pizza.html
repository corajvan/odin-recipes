Homemade Pizza

Description

Using bread flour for the best consistency, this is the best homemade pizza dough you'll find out there. You'll have the flexibility of using your own delicious toppings to make a great tasting pizza for the family to share.

Pizza Dough Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups (355 ml) warm water (105°F-115°F)
  • 1 package (2 1/4 teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • 3 3/4 cups (490g) bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil (omit if cooking pizza in a wood-fired pizza oven)
  • 2 teaspoons kosher salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
